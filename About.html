WHO WE ARE


We, Group Three, are a group of web designers of the Tay Tung High School Batch of 2022. Taught by our one and only amazing ICT teacher, sir Clyde Santes, we have created this website using the knowledge we have acquired from attending our classes, as well as researching on more ways on how we can make an enjoyable and attractive website. We are greatly privileged to have had the opportunity to learn about Web Design during our stay here in Tay Tung. In this page, we will introduce ourselves tell you more about who worked behind the scenes of making Istacion.
